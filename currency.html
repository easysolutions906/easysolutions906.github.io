Frequently Asked Questions

How often are exchange rates updated?

Rates are updated every hour from the European Central Bank via Frankfurter. Weekday rates reflect the latest ECB reference rates; weekend queries return Friday's closing rates.

Which currencies are supported?

Over 30 currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CAD, AUD, CHF, CNY, INR, BRL, MXN, KRW, SEK, NOK, DKK, PLN, TRY, ZAR, NZD, SGD, HKD, THB, MYR, PHP, IDR, CZK, HUF, ILS, and more.

How far back do historical rates go?

Historical exchange rate data is available from January 4, 1999 (the introduction of the Euro) to present.

Is there a free tier for the API?

Yes. The Pay Per Use plan starts at $0.003 per call with no monthly commitment. This web tool is completely free with no signup required.
